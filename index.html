Education · Live

Career Spotlight Day

A complete student booking and allocation platform for school career events. Students sign up with their school username, choose their career preferences, and receive a personalised timetable — all automatically.

  • Arbor MIS integration — import students directly
  • Preference-based allocation engine
  • Staff dashboard with live sign-up tracking
  • Automated PDF timetables per form
  • Session registers and booking exports
  • Form tutor portal for class monitoring
